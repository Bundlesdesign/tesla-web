Our Mission

Accelerating Sustainable Energy

Tesla's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy through increasingly affordable electric vehicles, energy generation, and storage products. We believe the faster the world stops relying on fossil fuels and moves towards a zero-emission future, the better.
